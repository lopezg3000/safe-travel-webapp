Safe Travel was developed in 2021 during the Kennesaw State/Digital Crafts Flex June Cohort with a goal to help people make informed travel decisions. After a year and a half of pandemic stress, Gabrielle, Julia and Carmen wanted to provide people, who are ready for a change of scenery, a place to find Covid-19 stats about their potential travel destination. Thus "Safe Travel" was born.
